About

One tool, done properly

Convertersolutions is a single-purpose unit converter covering length, weight, temperature, volume, speed, area, digital storage, pressure and energy. It used to also handle currency and time zone conversion; both were removed so the site could stay focused on one job and do it well, rather than spreading a handful of features thin.

01What it does

The converter runs entirely in your browser using fixed, standard conversion factors — the kind published in engineering and scientific references, not estimates. Every category converts through a single base unit (meters for length, liters for volume, pascals for pressure, and so on), and temperature is handled with its proper offset formulas rather than a simple ratio, since Celsius, Fahrenheit and Kelvin don't share a zero point.

02Why it's free

The site is supported by display advertising rather than subscriptions or accounts. No login is required to use any part of the converter, and no personal information is collected to serve the tool itself. Details of what limited data is collected automatically (and why) are in the Privacy Policy.

03What we're not

This is a reference and planning tool, not a certified measurement instrument. For engineering tolerances, medical dosing, legal filings, or anything where a rounding error has real consequences, verify the figure against an authoritative source or calibrated equipment. See the Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

04Feedback

If you spot an incorrect conversion factor or a category you think is missing, that feedback is genuinely useful — the goal is a small tool that gets the basics right rather than one that tries to cover everything.
