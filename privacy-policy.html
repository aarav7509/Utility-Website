📥 Information We Collect

Convertersolutions is designed to be a privacy-friendly tool. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use our service.

The following types of non-personal data may be collected automatically when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • General geographic location (country/region level only, not precise location)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)

This data is collected automatically via server logs and third-party analytics/advertising tools, and is used in aggregate form only.

📢 Google AdSense & Cookies

Convertersolutions uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences and allow advertisers to show relevant ads.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ad Settings: www.google.com/settings/ads
  • NAI opt-out: www.networkadvertising.org/choices
  • YourAdChoices: www.youradchoices.com

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data held about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data for advertising purposes
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format

Since we do not directly collect personal data, most of these rights apply to the third-party services we use (such as Google). You can exercise your rights over Google's data at myaccount.google.com.

For EU/EEA residents, these rights are provided under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For California residents, rights are provided under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
